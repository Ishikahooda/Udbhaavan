GREENOVATION

In a bid to transform our world to a better version of itself, United Nations has piloted its campaign of 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Taking this forward, we want the future generation to be well informed and concerned so that they can contribute their innovative, green and sustainable solutions to the posed environmental threats around the planet. This event will expose the kids to the concept of sustainable development with the sense of achievement as they solve real life case studies applying their innovation and problem solving skills.

JUNKYARD WARS

Scrap consists of materials left over from product manufacturing and consumption, such as parts of vehicles, building supplies, and surplus materials. This event aims to develop Critical Thinking, Creativity, Innovation and Problem Solving in kids. It aim to appraise how kids will be able to use these skills and create something out of scrap together as a Team.

Event details...

LEAKY PIPES/DOWNSTREAM

Natural Gas is an essential power resource of world, processed with crude oil. It is then distributed over large distances through a network of underground pipelines and make the transport of natural gas easy, time efficient and cost effective. These pipeline networks come with a pertinent threat - breakage of pipeline. How will things be, if one day a gas pipeline starts leaking?
