Science Quiz

Test your knowledge of science facts and applications of scientific principles. It’s going to fun as it will include questions, images and much more. This will be organized by QuizCraft, ‘Why So Curious?’

Theme- Science & Technology

Rules and Regulations:
1. Only ONE team from a school can participate in a particular event.
2. There should be no more than 3 participants in a team.
3. Inter School teams will not be allowed to participate.

Structure for the quiz:
Prelims:
Written Quiz
Duration: 30 min
Result will be announced on-the-spot

Finals:
6 Highest scoring Teams will move to Finals In the event of tie, there will be tie-breaker questions
There will be 5 rounds in the final.
The final round will be a mix of Audio visual, rapid fire, challenge round etc.
The team with Highest Score at the end of 5 rounds will be declared WINNER.
Duration: 1 hr

There will be challenges and goodies for the audience as well.
